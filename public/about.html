About ParcelTracker

Our journey, mission, and the team behind India's trusted logistics partner

Our Story

Founded in 2025, ParcelTracker Logistics began as a small delivery service in Bangalore with just three vehicles and a big vision. Today, we've grown into one of India's most trusted logistics companies, serving over 500 cities across the country.

Our journey has been defined by innovation, customer-centric approach, and an unwavering commitment to excellence. We've embraced technology to transform traditional logistics into a seamless, transparent, and efficient experience for our customers.

With a network of over 200 distribution centers, 5000+ delivery vehicles, and a team of dedicated professionals, we continue to expand our reach and capabilities to serve the evolving needs of Indian businesses and consumers.

Our Mission

To provide reliable, efficient, and innovative logistics solutions that empower businesses and delight customers across India.

Our Vision

To be India's most trusted logistics partner by leveraging technology, sustainability, and customer-centric approaches.
